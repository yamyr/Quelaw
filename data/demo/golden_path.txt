MEMORANDUM ON DUTY OF CARE AND RELATED AUTHORITIES

1. The applicable test for a duty of care in negligence in Singapore is the
   single two-stage test of proximity and policy laid down in Spandeck
   Engineering (S) Pte Ltd v Defence Science & Technology Agency [2007] SGCA 37.

2. The recognition of novel heads of damage was considered by the Court of
   Appeal in ACB v Thomson Medical Pte Ltd [2017] SGCA 20.

3. Counsel further relies on the decision in Tan Ah Kow v Singapore Airlines
   [2025] SGHC 999, which is said to have extended the duty of care to purely
   commercial relationships.

4. The statutory basis for the claim is section 14 of the Civil Law Act, read
   with Order 9 Rule 6 of the Rules of Court.
